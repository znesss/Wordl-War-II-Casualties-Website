Guide:

Select a Country from the map. Use the menu to focus.

The map and colors correspond to the borders during WWII. Therefore for instance, choosing Russia, Ukrain, Azerbaijan,...; all result the details of Soviet Union.
Hovering over the map, provides information of the country.

Then Scroll down the page to see The details as well as a Pie Chart.

The Pie chart shows the AVERAGE casualty in each 1-year period.
Hover to see the year related to each slice of pie.
